1) Benchmark



Identify and keep an eye on the competitors.



What's new in my product ?


+ Advantages / differences with similar apps ?


+ Position towards the market ?


Do I have a chance to find my audience ?

2) Have a Strategy



When to launch ?
NO travel apps during January ! 


During the first 30 days, apps are in New Apps category = Great way to be on top of the charts even with a few downloads !


Blog about it before and after launch.


Plan the upcoming releases (date + new features) to retain users' interest.


Find who are your users, and focus on them. Even having a few fanatic users is great, they won't count what they'll spend and will promote your apps well.

3) Understand the app store "Funnel"


  1. App Store Optimisation (ASO)
    (More details in section 4)
  2. App appears in research
  3. User evaluates  App name + icon + rating
  4. Then  screenshots + reviews

4) Get to grips with ASO



App name is IMPORTANT ! NO accents, NO special characters !


Embed keywords in app name


Embed keywords in app description
Avoid nonsense list of keywords in the description !!!


Use Google Adwords Keyword Tool.

5) First impression counts



Users are doing snap decisions. So, here are the key points :


- Take good screenshots, with descriptive overlays.


- Make a short video (around 30s long, less than one minute).


- Keep the description short. 10 concise lines full of keywords are enough, most users won't read 50 lines of text on their phones...

6) Understand the app store owners


In order to get more chances to be featured (= Holy Grail).



Google != Apple != Amazon != Samsung


Follow platform's design guidelines !

For example on Android, do not copy iPhone design, Google do not like it at all.

Use platform's new APIs or APIs that other platforms do not have (NFC, rich notifications, ...).

7) Spread the word



- Have a media plan (blogs, HN, tech websites...).
A good video is important.


- Do not expect any guarantee from media.


- Post the video on Youtube !


- Of course, use Social Networks. Facebook page, etc...


- Ask for feedbacks on platforms such as Reddit, forums, ...
You will get improvement ideas + potential users at the same time !

8) Mobile advertising



Spend huge money or do not spend anything at all.


9) Cross Promotion



Add links to your other apps on every app you publish.
It really works and it is free.


10) Produce Quality apps



Obvious but it is needed to have your apps auto-promoted by your users.


Bonus ! Miscellaneous own tips
(Warning : these are highly subjective)


Obvious : make appear a rating popup after a few launches.
Add a "rate app" button in the settings.
Ratings are the KEY (numbers + level). I personnaly prefer more ratings, even bad ones, than a 5/5 stars app with fewer ratings.


Add a contact button in the settings.
A quick answer to a user's question grants you a 5 star rating and a great review most of the time !

Remove the splashscreen after some launches.

Replies to bad ratings on the store, most of the time these are due to technical problems or incomprehension.

If your app is just a demo and users need to buy the full app, tell it in the app's description because transparency is cool and it avoids users' unexpected reactions.
